Pay a TV license fine

Use this service to pay a fine for not having a TV Licence when one was required.

Your notice of fine letter will tell you how much you have to pay and when you must pay it by.

This page is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

How to pay

You need your fine reference number and a debit or credit card. If youâ€™ve lost your notice of fine, contact the court where you received the fine.

You must pay by the date given in your notice of fine. The court might reject your payment if enforcement action has already started.
